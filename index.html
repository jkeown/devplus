About Dev Plus

I am (like most) a self-taught programmer. I enjoy creating websites and web apps so much I decided to start my small business.

As I've learned web development and spoken with small business owners, I've realized that a lot of small businesses are paying more for services they really do not need. My mission is to accurately assess your business needs and not upsell you on unnecessary extras.
